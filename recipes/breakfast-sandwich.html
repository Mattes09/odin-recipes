Breakfast Sandwich



Ingredients

  • sourdough
  • 2 eggs
  • tomato
  • mozzarela
  • balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Toast a couple slices of sourdough
  2. cramble an egg and slide it onto the toasted bread.
  3. Top with a thick tomato slice, a thick slice of fresh mozzarella, and a generous drizzle of balsamic glaze
